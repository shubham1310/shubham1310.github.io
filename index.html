Hi, I am working at Visa Research as a Sr. Software Engineer in the Risk Modelling team. Before that I was a master's student at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), USA and my master's thesis was focused on using landmark features for clothing retrieval. Previous to this, I completed by Bachelor in Technology degree from the department of Computer Science and Engineering at IIT Kanpur, India.

I hail from Vidisha, MP, India. My research interests lies in Computer Vision, Deep Learning and Algorithms. I am also fascinated by Game theory.


I worked in Nvidia as a summer intern in 2018 in the Drive-IX team focussing on using facial landmarks. In 2017, I worked on a research project at Adobe, India on High Resolution Form Image Parsing.

In 2016, I worked under Prof. Yoshua Bengio, MILA on sound generation where we worked on stacked GRU models and published the paper on the same in ICLR.
